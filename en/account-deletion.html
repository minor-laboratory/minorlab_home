Account Deletion

1. Delete directly from the app

BookLab App → More Tab → Account Management → Account Withdrawal

⚠️ Important Notice
When you delete your account, all data (reading records, notes, goals, etc.) will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

2. Request via email

Please fill out the form below to request account deletion

If you have any questions, please contact danny@minorlab.com.

Send Email

Processing Procedure

  • Account verification email will be sent within 3 days of receiving the request
  • Account and data will be completely deleted when you click the deletion confirmation link in the email
  • Deletion completion notification email will be sent
